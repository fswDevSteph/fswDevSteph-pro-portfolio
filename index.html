Hey! I'm Steph.

I originally started in IT because I had moved to an area where I could not speak the language - silly, right? I know. Finding employment took a lot of work, but I managed. I took a few courses to speak the language and approached many small businesses, hoping to be their social media manager. After countless weird looks and numerous rejections, someone took a chance on me.

Social Media management was a blast, but I knew I was meant for more. I started educating myself through YouTube videos and began software Testing. Projects were small until preparations met opportunity, and I got to work on the most significant data migration project in New Brunswick's history! This was a great experience, but always having the itch to create and being passionate about life-long learning, my interests turned to Software Development.

As of October 23rd, 2023, I am enrolled in a Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp and couldn't be more excited about what lies ahead.
